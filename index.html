01 · The Challenge

Great at the work.
Terrible at finding it.

SkillPrax had the skills, the track record, and a clear offer. But generating a consistent pipeline of high-intent, decision-ready prospects was a full-time job layered on top of client delivery.

Manual prospecting meant scrolling Meta Ad Library for hours, researching companies with no signal on whether they actually needed help — then sending generic outreach into the void.

The result? Zero qualified meetings. Zero predictable pipeline. Revenue entirely dependent on referrals and luck. What they needed was a system that surfaced brands at the exact moment they were in pain — and wrote the outreach to match, automatically.

  • 3–4 Hours Lost Daily

    Scrolling the Meta Ad Library by hand. No scoring. No prioritisation. Pure guesswork on who to contact.

  • No Signal = No Urgency

    Reaching out to brands with no idea if they're bleeding spend. Generic emails get ignored in under 2 seconds.

  • 0 Qualified Meetings

    Weeks of effort resulting in zero booked calls with decision-ready prospects. Relegated to word-of-mouth survival.

  • No Pipeline Visibility

    No way to predict next month's revenue. Growth stalled. The manual prospecting bottleneck was the constraint.
