01 · The Challenge

Great at the work.
Terrible at finding it.

SkillPrax had the skills, the track record, and a clear offer. But generating a consistent pipeline of high-intent, decision-ready prospects was a full-time job layered on top of client delivery. Manual prospecting meant scrolling Meta Ad Library for hours, researching companies with no signal on whether they actually needed help — then sending generic outreach into the void.

The result? Zero qualified meetings. Zero predictable pipeline. Revenue entirely dependent on referrals and luck. What they needed was a system that surfaced brands at the exact moment they were in pain — and wrote the outreach to match, automatically.

Pain Points

  • 3–4 hours of manual research daily
    Scrolling the Meta Ad Library by hand. No scoring. No prioritisation. Pure guesswork.
  • No signal = no urgency in outreach
    Reaching out to brands with no idea if they're bleeding spend. Generic emails get ignored in under 2 seconds.
  • 0 qualified meetings per week
    Weeks of effort. Zero booked calls with decision-ready prospects.
  • No pipeline visibility
    No way to predict next month's revenue. Growth stalled. Consulting bottleneck.
02 · The Solution

AdRadar Deployed.

An autonomous 7-step intelligence pipeline running at 6 AM every morning — delivering pre-scored, pre-enriched, pre-written prospect reports directly into SkillPrax's inbox before the first meeting of the day.

01

Scrape

Scans Meta Ad Library daily for UK eCommerce brands actively running ads. Captures ad count, creative age, and spend signals in real time — then identifies underperforming campaigns using 5 live signals.

Meta Ad Library API
02

Filter

Removes brands running under 30 days (not yet fatigued) and eliminates enterprise accounts outside SkillPrax's target range. Only qualified signals survive.

Smart Filter Engine
03

Enrich

Finds founder name, verified email, LinkedIn profile, employee count, and funding status. No cold outreach to gatekeepers — straight to the decision maker.

Apollo.io
04

Traffic Analysis

Pulls paid traffic dependency % and monthly visit volume. Brands at 60%+ paid dependency are desperate — ads stop and revenue collapses. Maximum urgency.

Similarweb
05

Score (5 Signals)

Runs every brand against 5 live signals: Creative Fatigue, Desperation Testing, Copy Quality, Paid Dependency, Team Size. Outputs HOT / WARM / COLD tier instantly.

AI Scoring Engine
06

Write

Generates 3 hyper-personalised emails per HOT lead — each referencing the brand's exact ad weakness. Not templates. Not generic. Specific, verifiable, converting.

Gemini AI
07

Export + Launch
